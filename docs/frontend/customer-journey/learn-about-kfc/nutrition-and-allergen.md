# Nutrition and Allergen

This page covers standalone nutrition and allergen discovery outside the PDP flow.

The latest prototype source currently documents nutrition and allergen information as a PDP bottom sheet, not as a standalone browse or search experience.

For the current implemented journey, use:

[Open PDP Nutrition and Allergen documentation](/docs/frontend/customer-journey/preview-product-and-customize/nutrition-and-allergen)

## What This Feature Is

Standalone Nutrition and Allergen would support customers who want to research products before selecting a menu item. This is different from the PDP sheet, where the customer is already reviewing a specific product.

## Why This Step Is Designed This Way

Some customers start with dietary research rather than product selection, so this content needs a standalone route.
