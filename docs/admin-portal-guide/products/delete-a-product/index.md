# Delete a Product

## What this guide covers

Permanently removes a product from the system when it is discontinued or was created in error.

## Steps

**Step 1:** Navigate to the **Products** section using the left navigation menu.

**Step 2:** Find the product you want to delete. You can search by Product Name or Product Code.

**Step 3:** Click the three-dot menu next to the product name, then select **Delete**.

![Step 2](./img/step-2.png)

**Step 4:** A confirmation modal will appear showing all the areas of the system where this product is used. Review this carefully to ensure you are deleting the correct item.

**Step 5:** Click the red **Delete** button to permanently delete the product.

![Step 3](./img/step-3.png)

## Notes

:::caution
Deleting a product is permanent and cannot be undone. The product will be removed from all menus and associated areas of the catalog.
:::

:::tip
You can search products by Product Name or Product Code to quickly find the item you want to delete.
:::

:::caution
Click **Cancel** if you do not want to proceed with deletion.
:::
