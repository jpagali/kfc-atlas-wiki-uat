# Edit/Delete a Beneficiary

## What this guide covers

Updates or removes an existing beneficiary record, including its name, donation status, and associated stores.

## Steps

**Step 1:** Navigate to the **Stores** section using the left-hand navigation menu.

**Step 2:** Click the **Beneficiaries** tab at the top of the Stores page.

**Step 3:** Use the search box to find the beneficiary by **name**, or scroll through the list.

![Step 2](./img/step-2.png)

**Step 4:** Once you find the beneficiary, click the **three-dot menu** (•••) icon on the beneficiary's row to open the options menu.

### To Edit:

**Step 5:** Click **Edit** to open the beneficiary details form.

**Step 6:** Update the beneficiary fields as needed. All fields marked with * are required.

| Field | What to enter | Notes |
|-------|--------------|-------|
| **Beneficiary Name** * | Name of the charity or cause | e.g., “KFC Youth Foundation” |
| **Accepting Donations** | Toggle: Yes or No | Controls whether donations are active |
| **Associated Stores** | Select stores | Use search to add or remove stores from this beneficiary |

**Step 7:** Once changes are complete, click **Save** to update the beneficiary.

![Step 3](./img/step-3.png)

### To Delete:

**Step 5:** Click **Delete** from the options menu.

**Step 6:** Confirm the deletion in the modal that appears by clicking **Delete** again. This action cannot be undone.

:::caution
Deleting a beneficiary cannot be reversed. To temporarily disable a beneficiary, set **Accepting Donations** to **No** instead.
:::

:::caution
Clicking **Cancel** at any time discards unsaved changes (Edit mode only).
:::
