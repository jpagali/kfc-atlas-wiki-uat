# Copy a Choice

## What this guide covers

Duplicates an existing choice to quickly create similar selection slots for other bundles.

## Steps

**Step 1:** Navigate to the **Bundles** section using the left-hand navigation menu.

**Step 2:** Click the **Choices** tab at the top of the Bundles screen.

![Step 2](./img/step-2.png)

**Step 3:** Find the choice you want to copy by searching by Choice Name, Choice Code, Choice Display Name, or Catalog Tags.

**Step 4:** Click the **⋮** (three-dot menu) button in the same row as the choice, then select **Copy**.

![Step 3](./img/step-3.png)

**Step 5:** A form opens to create the new choice. You must enter a unique **Choice Code**.

![Step 4](./img/step-4.png)

**Step 6:** Adjust the **Choice Name** if needed. By default, it will be “Copy of [Original Name]” — update this to something descriptive.

**Step 7:** Review all other fields (Min/Max Quantity, Products, Variants, etc.). All settings from the original choice are inherited. Modify as needed.

**Step 8:** Click **Create Choice** to finish creating the duplicate choice.

:::tip
The copied choice includes all products and variants from the original. You can modify these before or after creation.
:::
