# Create a Choice

## What this guide covers

Creates a selection slot within a bundle (e.g., "Choose Your Side" or "Select Your Drink"), letting customers personalise their combo by selecting from available products.

## Steps

**Step 1:** Navigate to the **Bundles** section using the left-hand navigation menu.

**Step 2:** Click the **Choices** tab at the top of the Bundles screen.

![Step 2](./img/step-2.png)

**Step 3:** Click the **+ Create New Choice** button.

![Step 3](./img/step-3.png)

**Step 4:** Fill in the choice details:

| Field | What to enter | Notes |
|-------|--------------|-------|
| **Choice Code** * | Unique identifier | Use uppercase letters, numbers, and hyphens — e.g., `CHOICE-SIDE` |
| **Choice Name** * | Label shown to customers | e.g., "Choose Your Side", "Select Your Drink" |
| **Choice Display Name** | Shorter name for limited space | Defaults to Choice Name if left blank |
| **Min Quantity** | Minimum selections required | Set to `0` to make the choice optional |
| **Max Quantity** | Maximum selections allowed | e.g., `1` for single-select, `2` for dual-select |

**Step 5:** Add products and variants to this choice:

- Click the **Products** field and search for the product you want to include
- Select the product — a list of available variants appears
- Check the variants you want customers to be able to select (e.g., "Regular Fries", "Large Fries")
- Click **Add** to include this product

**Step 6:** Repeat Step 5 for any additional products or variants you want in this choice.

**Step 7:** Click **Create Choice** to save the choice.

![Step 4](./img/step-4.png)

:::tip
You can search for choices later by Choice Name, Choice Code, Choice Display Name, or Catalog Tags to quickly find what you need.
:::
