# 注文検索

## このガイドで扱う内容

このガイドでは、Byte Commerce Admin Portal で注文検索手順を説明します。

## 手順

**ステップ 1:** まず、こちらをクリックして Customer Support 画面に移動します。
**ステップ 2:** as many fields in each box (Order Information, Delivery Address and Customer Information) as you can to help narrow down your search for better results finding the order you are looking for を入力します。

![Step 2](./img/step-2.png)

**ステップ 3:** Once you have entered in all of the needed information, Click Search to reveal your findings.

![Step 3](./img/step-3.png)

**ステップ 4:** Once your search results appear, you will see several columns of information. You man need to scroll left to see more.

![Step 4](./img/step-4.png)

**Step 5a:** To see more detailed information click the order number.

![Step 5](./img/step-5.png)

**Step 5b:** To find the exact info you are looking for choose from this list by clicking on the 3 vertical dots.

![Step 6](./img/step-6.png)

**ステップ 6:** To go through the list in order こちらをクリック.

![Step 7](./img/step-7.png)

**ステップ 7:** To resend the customers receipt こちらをクリック to open a drawer to verify their email address.

![Step 8](./img/step-8.png)

**ステップ 8:** To send the receipt こちらをクリック.

![Step 9](./img/step-9.png)

**ステップ 9:** To go through the list in order こちらをクリック.

![Step 10](./img/step-10.png)

**ステップ 10:** To start the refund process こちらをクリック.

![Step 11](./img/step-11.png)

**ステップ 11:** To go through the list in order こちらをクリック.

![Step 12](./img/step-12.png)

**ステップ 12:** To issue a refund you must give a purpose to it by choosing from the list example above.

**ステップ 12:** To offer a partial refund you will need to enter in the amount being offered here.

**ステップ 13:** 完了したら、this ボタン will become active for you to click and Issue the refund。

**ステップ 13:** To issue a refund you must give a purpose to it by choosing from the list example above.

**ステップ 14:** 完了したら、this ボタン will become active for you to click and Issue the refund。

![Step 13](./img/step-13.png)
