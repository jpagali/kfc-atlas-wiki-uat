Passwordless Authentication

Removes password dependency and enables OTP login for faster, more secure customer authentication, especially in high-friction mobile flows.

Expected impactHigher login conversion and reduced password reset friction for customers returning to order.
Where it fitsMarkets focused on conversion, repeat ordering, and simplifying authentication support overhead.
Coming Soon
Business problemPasswords create login abandonment and customer support friction during checkout and re-engagement.
How to use itEnable OTP-based authentication and configure the login experience for supported flows.
Best-fit marketsTeams prioritizing reduced friction in sign-in and better mobile conversion performance.
